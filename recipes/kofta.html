Kofta Recipes

Traditionally, kofta kebab is usually made with ground lamb or with a mix of ground beef and lamb.

However, I made my own version by using just ground beef and it is SO good.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg ground beef (use ground beef with 10% fat; I don't recommend using extra lean ground beef; otherwise, the kofta will turn out dry)
  • ½ cup parsley
  • 1 ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • 1 onion minced
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 white bun

Steps

  1. Chop the parsley and soak the bun in water until it becomes mushy then take it out and squeeze the water out of it
  2. In a bowl, mix all the ingredients until well combined.
  3. take a handful of the meat mixture and mold it on a wooden skewer. Repeat the process until all the meat is finish.
  4. Heat the grill then place kebabs on it and grill for about 7-8 minutes on each side after that remove from the grill and serve hot
