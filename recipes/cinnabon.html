Cinnabon Recipes

You have got to try this Cinnabon recipe.

The first time I made these rolls, I thought of how much money I could save by making my own!

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1 cup warm milk (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • ⅓ cup margarine, melted
  • 4 ½ cups bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 ½ teaspoons bread machine yeast

Filling:

  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ⅓ cup butter, softened

Icing:

  • 1 ½ cups confectioners' sugar
  • ¼ cup butter, softened
  • 1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Place milk, eggs, margarine, flour, salt, white sugar, and yeast in the pan of a bread machine
  2. Select dough cycle; press Start.
  3. When dough has doubled in size, turn it out onto a lightly floured surface.
  4. Cover it with a kitchen towel or plastic wrap and let it rest for 10 minutes.
  5. Roll dough on a lightly floured surface to a 16x21-inch rectangle.
  6. Combine brown sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl
  7. Spread softened butter over the dough, then sprinkle cinnamon-sugar mixture evenly over top.
  8. Starting at the longer end, roll up the dough; cut into 12 rolls.
  9. Place rolls in a lightly greased 9x13-inch baking pan
  10. Cover and let rise until nearly doubled, about 30 minutes.
  11. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  12. Bake rolls in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 15 minutes.
  13. Beat confectioners' sugar, butter, cream cheese, vanilla, and salt until creamy.
  14. Spread icing on warm rolls before serving.
