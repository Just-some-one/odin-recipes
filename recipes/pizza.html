Pizza Recipes

A simple bonus pepperoni trick infuses every bite of this pizza with extra pepperoni flavor.

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 16 ounces pizza dough, store-bought or homemade (this recipe makes enough dough for 2 pizzas)
  • 18 to 20 slices pepperoni
  • 12 ounces mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano, optional
  • Flour for rolling and shaping dough

Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (no salt)
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon granulated onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 500°F (If you are using a pizza stone, preheat it in the oven for at least 20 minutes so it is nice and hot as well.)
  2. Make the sauce:

    stir together the ingredients. The sauce recipe makes just enough for one large pizza.

  3. Roll out the dough:

    Roll out dough on a lightly floured surface. If it's hard to roll, let it rest for 5 minutes so it can come to room temperature.

    For a large pizza, I like to roll my dough into about a 14-inch diameter circle.

  4. Add the toppings:
    1. Transfer the dough to a lightly dusted pizza peel. Alternatively, fit it into a large cast-iron.
    2. Add sauce in a light layer all over the pizza, leaving about 1/4-inch crust around the edges.
    3. Chop half of the pepperoni and sprinkle it over the sauce.
    4. Top the pizza with grated cheese and the rest of the pepperoni.
    5. Season with black pepper.
  5. Cook the pizza:
      If you're using a pizza stone
    1. carefully slide pizza into the center of the preheated pizza stone. Cook for 6 minutes
    2. rotate the pizza halfway so it cooks evenly.
    3. Cook for another 6 to 8 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and charred in spots.
      If you're using a skillet
    1. press the dough into a cast iron skillet and add toppings.
    2. Place the skillet over a high heat burner for 2 minutes to get it preheated and get the crust cooking right away.
    3. transfer to a 500 °F oven and bake for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
